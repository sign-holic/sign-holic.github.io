Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-01 (latest revision)

This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile apps and services developed and operated by SignHolic (operated by Shinho Kang). We respect your privacy and outline our data practices below.

1. Apps Covered

This policy applies jointly to the following apps published by SignHolic on Google Play and other stores:

Brikar Sudoku Zen SignWord SignDoku SignMath SignBlockPack SignMatchPack SignLogicPack SnapBlock MemoryFlip BreathMate DayHolic Other SignHolic apps

Each app may have slightly different functions and data scope; differences are noted inline below where applicable.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Automatically Collected

  • Advertising Identifier (Google Advertising ID, AAID) — for personalized ads (AdMob)
  • Device information — model, OS version, screen resolution, language
  • Usage statistics — session length, stage progression, crash reports
  • Network info — IP address (country/region level), carrier info (for ad delivery optimization)

2.2 Information You Provide

  • In-app purchase data — payment token and product ID issued by Google Play (we never receive your card number; only the receipt is verified)
  • Google Play Games account — only if you opt in to leaderboards/achievements
  • Email inquiries — content you send us directly

2.3 Stored Locally on Your Device (not transmitted)

  • Game progress (cleared stages, scores, owned items)
  • In-app currency balances (coins, prestige coins, etc.)
  • Unlocked content (Brikar 9-Ball collection, category packs, etc.)
  • User preferences (language, sound, vibration)

Local data is never transmitted to external servers and is removed when you uninstall the app.

3. Purposes of Use

  • Provide and maintain game functionality
  • Display and measure personalized ads (AdMob)
  • Process in-app purchases and grant entitlements
  • Analyze errors/crashes to improve service stability
  • Respond to user inquiries
  • Comply with applicable laws and prevent fraud

5. Data Retention

  • Advertising ID & device info — until you stop using the service or reset your advertising ID
  • In-app purchase data — retained per Google Play policy; we discard receipt tokens after verification
  • Crash logs — auto-deleted after 90 days (Firebase default)
  • Local data — until you uninstall the app

6. Your Rights

You may exercise the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access and copy
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure (deletion)
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Reset advertising identifier (Device Settings → Google → Ads)
  • Opt out of personalized ads (Device Settings → Google → Ads → "Opt out of Ads Personalization")

Send requests to signholic.dev@gmail.com; we respond within 14 days.

Local data can be deleted instantly by uninstalling the app or using "Settings → Reset Data" within the app.

7. Children's Privacy (Under 13)

Our apps are intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

If a parent or guardian believes their child's data has been collected, please contact signholic.dev@gmail.com and we will promptly delete it.

Ad delivery complies with COPPA (US Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and GDPR-K (EU children's protection), and may be served as non-personalized ads where applicable.

8. Data Security

  • HTTPS encryption for data transmission
  • Least-privilege access controls
  • Compliance with third-party providers' security standards (Google, etc.)
  • Anonymization of analytics data (e.g., crash logs)

This policy may be updated due to legal changes or service improvements. Material changes will be communicated through in-app notices or this page.
